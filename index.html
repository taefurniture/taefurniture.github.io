Designs

O.I.E.

$22

O.I.E. is our introductory chair that allows our users a piece of beautiful furniture that doesn't break the bank. Designed to fold completely flat, this chair is able to be placed in almost any location to store it away, and folds up with almost no effort.

Manufatured using the most cutting edge wood fabrication techniques, this chair is built to last. Made from the highest grade birch plywood, O.I.E. is incredibly affordable to make. Birch plywood is also an incredibly versitile material, allowing O.I.E. to be stained in dozens of colors and finishes providing you the exact chair that you need.

A high quality chair, shouldn't come with a high pricetag. Because of that, O.I.E. is only $22 allowing our customers to buy multiple to really finish off their new home with comfortable, beautiful chairs.

Colors

The O.I.E. comes in a variety of colors and rope combinations as well as a custom option. Since the O.I.E. is so easily manufactured, it can just as easily be painted or stained any color combination of your choosing.

Naked

Pure, unadulterated birch plywood with a layer of polyacrylic to keep your chair looking pristine for years to come.

Modern

A clean, modern approach to the chair. Stained in a light grey and finished with clear sealant and featuring a complementary colored logo.

Warmth

Rich, golden brown stain with a clear sealant. A classic color that will fit almost all spaces.

Royal Midnight

Dark, rich, and classy. A beautiful dark brown stain with a clear sealant will leave your friends wondering if you are indeed royalty...

About

Founded in the spring of 2017, TAE Furniture began, like many companies, from a need for a product. Being students at Olin College of Engineering, the team was always in need of more furniture to be able to host friends in their dorm rooms, but the right furniture wasn't out there. All the other furniture was either too cheaply constructed, to ugly, or too expensive. The team wanted to change that, and thus TAE was founded.
